# The Travelers Companies

The Travelers Companies, Inc. (NYSE: TRV) is a leading United States property and casualty (P&C) insurance carrier providing auto, home, and business insurance through approximately 15,000 independent agents and brokers and select direct-to-consumer channels across the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and the Republic of Ireland. Travelers is a component of the Dow Jones Industrial Average, generated approximately $48.83 billion in revenue in 2025, holds roughly $143.7 billion in total assets, and employs approximately 34,000 people.

## APIs

Travelers operates a developer portal at [developer.travelers.com](https://developer.travelers.com) that surfaces partner APIs intended for appointed agents, agency-management-system (AMS) vendors, and integration partners. The portal is implemented on Salesforce Experience Cloud and is gated — individual API specifications, endpoints, rate limits, and pricing are not publicly published, so no per-API entries are profiled here. Public-facing developer artifacts (OpenAPI documents, SDK packages, Postman collections, CLI binaries, changelogs) are not currently available.

The `apis:` array in `apis.yml` is therefore empty. The developer portal, GitHub organization, and named branded products are captured as `common` properties.

## Branded Products

- **IntelliDrive** — 90-day smartphone telematics program (iOS 14+, Android 10+) scoring personal-auto driving behavior for renewal discounts of up to 30%.
- **IntelliDrivePlus** — continuous telematics variant available in select states.
- **MyTravelers** — customer self-service portal and mobile experience for personal-lines policies, billing, and claims.

## GitHub Organization

[github.com/travelers](https://github.com/travelers) ("Travelers Technology", Hartford, CT, opensource@travelers.com) exposes two public repositories — `KubUI` (JavaScript, last updated 2019) and `cloud-automation` (Python fork of Dynatrace observability content, last updated 2023). The organization publishes no language SDKs or developer tools.
